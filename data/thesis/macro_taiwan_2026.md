# Taiwan equity macro context — 2026 outlook

## Index level
TAIEX traded in a 18,000–22,500 range over the past 12 months,
with a clear uptrend driven by the AI hardware cycle. Concentration
risk remains elevated — TSMC + 8 large-cap semis account for ~35% of
index weight. Drawdowns of 8–12% have been routine when foreign
institutional outflows exceed NTD 100B in a single week.

## Foreign flows
- **Foreign net buying** in TWSE has been the dominant intraday
  driver in 2026. A simple but reliable signal: 5-day rolling
  foreign net > NTD 50B is positive; persistent net selling beyond
  3 weeks usually precedes a 5–8% pullback.
- **Investment trust (本土投信)** flows are slower-moving and
  often add convicted bids to mid-cap tech and biotech.

## Macro overlays
- **TWD/USD**: in 30.5–31.8 band. Sharp TWD strength historically
  caps tech earnings momentum (~60% USD revenue exposure for the
  semi complex).
- **Fed policy**: dovish surprise → yield curve flattening → higher
  multiples for long-duration tech earnings. Hawkish surprise → mean
  revert toward 16x median PE band.
- **CN stimulus pulse**: positive cyclically for upstream / commodity
  names but mixed for the AI cluster.

## Sector posture (March 2026)
- **Overweight**: leading-edge logic (TSMC), CoWoS/HBM ecosystem,
  AI server ODMs.
- **Marketweight**: memory (DRAM cycle late), networking IC.
- **Underweight**: pure-play traditional industrial, financials
  exposed to long-duration domestic real-estate.
