# 2454 MediaTek — Neutral stance (March 2026)

## Stance
Neutral. The Dimensity 9400 ramp into mid-tier Android flagships is
positive for 1H26 revenue, but the pace of share gains in connectivity
and ASIC has not yet justified a re-rating above the 5-year median PE
of 16x.

## Bull case
- Tencent / Bytedance custom ASIC tape-outs picking MediaTek over
  Marvell could add USD 1.5–2.5B in incremental revenue by 2H27.
- Wi-Fi 7 + 5G RedCap penetration in IoT modules accelerating.
- Auto SoC pipeline (cockpit + ADAS) entering volume in 2027.

## Bear case
- Qualcomm aggressively defending premium Android share.
- Apple modem in-housing pressure trickling into other RF / connectivity
  share losses.
- Margin pressure from 4nm wafer cost vs ASP discipline in mid-tier.

## Watch list
- Quarterly **mobile communication revenue mix** (target: keep > 55%).
- **Custom silicon design-win cadence** disclosed in conf calls.
- **Operating margin** holding above 18% on a TTM basis.
